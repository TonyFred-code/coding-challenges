Challenge 2: Savings Goal Tracker

Summary: You want to save a certain amount of money and want to track how many months it will take to reach your savings goal.

Write a function called calculateSavingsMonths that takes three parameters:

  1. savingsGoal (number) - The amount of money you want to save (in dollars).
  2. monthlySavings (number) - The amount of money you can save per month (in dollars).
  3. interestRate (number) - The annual interest rate on your savings account (in percentage).

The function should calculate and return the number of months it will take for you to reach your savings goal, considering your monthly savings and the interest earned on your savings account.
